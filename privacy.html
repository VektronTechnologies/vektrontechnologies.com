Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

Vektron Technologies, LLC ("Vektron," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and your rights regarding your data. This policy applies to all users of our website at vektrontechnologies.com and any related products, software, or services we provide.

By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you disagree with our practices, please discontinue use of our services and contact us to request data deletion.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories when you use our services:

Account & Identity Information

  • Full name and display name
  • Email address
  • Business name and role (for enterprise accounts)
  • Password (stored in hashed, non-reversible form — we never store plaintext passwords)
  • Account preferences and settings

Payment Information

  • Payment card details (credit/debit card number, expiration date, CVV) — collected and processed exclusively by our designated third-party payment processor. Vektron never stores full card numbers on our servers.
  • Billing address and postal code
  • Transaction history and subscription status

Usage & Platform Data

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, referral URLs, timestamps
  • Service usage activity: features accessed, sessions, and file uploads or exports associated with your account
  • Device information: device type, screen resolution, hardware identifiers
  • Crash reports and error logs for platform diagnostics

Communications

  • Emails and messages you send to us for support, consulting inquiries, or general contact
  • Records of support interactions

Information Provided Voluntarily

  • Survey responses and feedback
  • Information provided during consulting engagements or project scoping

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Creating and managing your account, providing access to our products and services, processing payments, and fulfilling hardware and consulting orders.
  • Communication: Sending transactional emails (receipts, subscription confirmations, password resets), service announcements, and responding to support requests.
  • Platform Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve features, fix bugs, and enhance user experience. We may use aggregated, de-identified data for this purpose.
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, unauthorized access, and abuse of our platform.
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting our obligations under applicable laws, responding to lawful requests from government authorities, and enforcing our Terms of Service.
  • Marketing (with consent): Sending product updates, newsletters, or promotional content. You may opt out of marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in each email or by contacting us directly.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We work with trusted third-party services to operate our platform. We share only the minimum information necessary for each service to function.

  • Payment Processing: All payment transactions are processed by a designated third-party payment processor. When you provide payment details, that information is transmitted directly to the processor's secure servers and is governed by their privacy policy. Vektron receives only non-sensitive payment metadata (such as last four digits, card type, expiration month/year, and transaction status).
  • Hosting & Infrastructure: Our platform is hosted on cloud infrastructure providers who process data on our behalf under data processing agreements. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Email Service Providers: We use email service providers to send transactional and marketing emails. These providers process your email address and message content solely on our behalf.
  • Analytics: We may use analytics tools to understand aggregate usage patterns. Where possible, we configure these tools to anonymize or pseudonymize data.

We may also disclose your information when required by law, in response to a valid legal process, or when necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Vektron Technologies, our users, or the public.

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and platform. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us provide and improve our services.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality such as authentication, session management, and security. These cannot be disabled without impairing your ability to use the platform.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences such as language selection, display settings, and account customizations.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users navigate our site and platform, allowing us to identify and improve areas of poor performance.
  • Payment Cookies: Set by our payment processor to enable secure payment processing and fraud prevention.

You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent certain features from working correctly. We honor browser-level "Do Not Track" signals where technically feasible.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/HTTPS
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Password hashing using secure, modern algorithms (e.g., bcrypt)
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure and application code

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take data protection seriously and apply reasonable safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus a reasonable period thereafter.
  • Financial transaction records are retained for a minimum of seven (7) years as required for tax and accounting compliance.
  • Support communications are retained for up to three (3) years.
  • Log and analytics data is typically retained in identifiable form for up to twelve (12) months, after which it may be aggregated or de-identified.

When you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, subject to retention requirements for financial records and legal compliance.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. We support the following rights for all users, regardless of jurisdiction:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Portability: Request your account data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in each email or by contacting us.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

We are aware of and committed to compliance with applicable U.S. state privacy laws, including where relevant. Users in states with specific consumer privacy rights (such as California's CCPA) may have additional rights under those frameworks. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@vektrontechnologies.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a minor, we will promptly delete it. If you believe we may have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Vektron Technologies is based in the United States, and your information is processed and stored in the United States. If you access our services from outside the U.S., your data may be transferred to and processed in the U.S., where privacy laws may differ from those in your country. By using our services, you consent to this transfer and processing.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, by sending you an email notification. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
