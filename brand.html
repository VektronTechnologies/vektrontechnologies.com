The Palette

Brand Colors

Vektron Violet is the one canonical brand color. Obsidian is the foundation, with Gunmetal and Dim White as supporting neutrals. The plasma and amber accents are atmospheric and functional respectively — never substitutes for the violet mark. No teal, green, or red appears anywhere.

The Typeface

Typography — Nyctos

Nyctos is VEKTRON's proprietary display typeface. Every capital V renders as the wing mark — that is intentional, a brand signature we call the Universal V Doctrine. Nyctos is used everywhere; Chakra Petch is an exception-only fallback for legal and VIN text.

Nyctos · Display

VEKTRON

Wordmarks, headlines, UI. Bold at 24px and up. Note the wing-glyph V — that is correct.

Nyctos · Body

Precision at the Binary Level.

Body, captions, microcopy. Regular and Light weights. The V wing appears inline by design.

The Standards

Usage Guidelines

The brand is locked. These are the rules that keep it consistent across every surface.

Do

  • Use the official logo files exactly as supplied.
  • Keep the mark in Vektron Violet (#53268E) or white.
  • Give the mark clear space and a dark, uncluttered backdrop.
  • Write the company name in all caps: VEKTRON.
  • Use the tagline "Precision at the Binary Level."™

Don't

  • Recolor, re-trace, or AI-generate the V mark.
  • Add stroke, glow, bevel, gradient, or shadow to the mark.
  • Skew, rotate, stretch, or distort any mark.
  • Place the mark on a busy or low-contrast background.
  • Use deprecated taglines or alternate spellings.
