About VEKTRON Technologies

Vektron Technologies, LLC is an automotive technology company founded in 2026 and headquartered in Fort Mill, South Carolina. We develop ECU calibration software, custom hardware solutions, and provide professional consulting services for the performance automotive market. Our flagship V-Tuning™ platform is currently in active development, designed to make ECU calibration more accessible for professional tuners and shops.
