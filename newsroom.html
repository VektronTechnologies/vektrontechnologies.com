Building in the Open

VEKTRON is a company built in public. This is the founder's running log — the milestones, the decisions, and the honest roadmap behind the Calibration Cloud. No press releases, no hype. Just the work, documented as it happens.

The Log

Milestones & Updates

The most recent updates appear first. Follow along on LinkedIn for the build-in-public thread as it unfolds.

  1. 2026 · Founding Beta

    The Founding Beta Opens for Reservations

    The V-Tuning founding beta is now taking seat reservations. We are targeting our first public beta this year — and the founding cohort gets in first, helps shape the platform, and locks in founder pricing.

    This is the moment the platform stops being a private build and starts being a product the professional tuning community can put their hands on.

  2. 2026 · Platform

    Multi-Language From Day One

    V-Tuning was designed for a global tuner community, not a single market. Multi-language support is built into the platform from the first release — because the people doing serious calibration work are everywhere, and the tools should meet them where they are.

  3. 2026 · Engineering

    Safety Systems, Built Into the Workflow

    The riskiest moment in tuning isn't the tune — it's the flash. One wrong move can brick a vehicle's computer. So validation, guardrails, and integrity checks aren't a feature we bolted on at the end; they're part of how the platform works at every step.

    The goal is simple: catch the mistake before it ever reaches the vehicle.

  4. 2026 · Intellectual Property

    Provisional Patent Filed With the USPTO

    VEKTRON filed a provisional patent on the calibration platform — the capability that makes V-Tuning different is now protected intellectual property, patent-pending. The company is 100% founder-owned.

    We talk about what the platform does. The specifics of how it does it stay where they belong: under protection.

  5. 2026 · Day 0

    VEKTRON Technologies Is Here

    VEKTRON Technologies, LLC was founded to close the gap between modern vehicles and the people who work on them. The tools serious tuners have lived with for years feel like they were designed in another decade. We are building the standard the industry should have had all along.

    The capability came first. The products are how it ships.
